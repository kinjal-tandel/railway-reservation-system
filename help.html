Help

• How to Book a Ticket?

Go to Inquiry page, select destination and date. Then check available trains and proceed to booking page.

• How to Check Train Schedule?

Open the Train Schedule page to view all available trains with time and date.

• How to Check PNR Status?

Go to PNR Status page and enter your Ticket ID to check your booking status.
